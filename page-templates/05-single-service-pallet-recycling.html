About Our Pallet Recycling Service

Pallets are one of the most commonly discarded items in logistics and distribution. But most can be repaired, recycled, or repurposed — keeping thousands of tons of wood out of landfills every year.

Our pallet recycling program handles everything: we pick up your used pallets on a schedule that works for you, sort them by condition, repair what's salvageable, and responsibly recycle the rest. You get certified documentation for every pickup.

How It Works

Four simple steps to sustainable pallet management.

1

Assessment

We evaluate your pallet volumes, types, and pickup frequency needs.

2

Collection

Scheduled pickups at your facility — we bring the trucks and equipment.

3

Sort & Repair

Pallets are sorted by condition. Repairable ones are restored to spec.

4

Recycle & Report

Non-repairable pallets are recycled. You receive certified documentation.

FAQ

What types of pallets do you accept? expand_more
We accept standard wooden pallets (GMA/CHEP sizes), non-standard wooden pallets, and plastic pallets of all sizes.
Do you pick up or do we need to deliver? expand_more
We pick up at your facility on a scheduled basis. You don't need to deliver anything.
Is there a minimum pallet quantity? expand_more
No strict minimum. We work with businesses generating anywhere from a few dozen to thousands of pallets per week.
