Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our recycling services.

What types of businesses do you work with? expand_more
We work with businesses of all sizes across every industry — from single-location retail stores to multi-site manufacturing operations. Our programs are custom-designed to fit your specific waste streams and volume.
How quickly can you set up a recycling program? expand_more
Most programs are up and running within 1–2 weeks of our initial assessment. For simple material pickups, we can often schedule service within a few business days.
Do you provide documentation for ESG reporting? expand_more
Yes. Every pickup includes weight tickets and certificates of recycling. We also provide monthly and annual summary reports with diversion rates, CO₂ savings calculations, and data formatted for ESG report integration.
What materials can you recycle? expand_more
We handle a wide range including electronics, metals, paper, plastics, pallets, textiles, batteries, chemicals, organics, light bulbs, and hazardous materials. If you're unsure about a specific material, contact us and we'll advise.
Is there a minimum volume requirement? expand_more
No strict minimums — we tailor our services to your needs. Whether you have a one-time cleanout or need recurring weekly pickups, we can design a cost-effective solution.
What areas do you serve? expand_more
We provide nationwide service across the United States. Our network of processing partners allows us to support businesses in any location, including multi-site operations with locations in different states.
